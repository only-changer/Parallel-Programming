=== \n Angola had an estimated total of 43 airports as of 2004, of which 31 had paved runways as of 2005. There is an [[Luanda International Airport|international airport at Luanda]]. International and domestic services are maintained by [[TAAG Angola Airlines]], [[Aeroflot]], [[British Airways]], [[Brussels Airlines]], [[Lufthansa]], [[Air France]], [[Cubana de Aviaci\u00f3n|Cubana]], [[Ethiopian Airlines]], [[Emirates (airline)|Emirates]], [[Delta Air Lines]], [[Royal Air Maroc]], [[Iberia Airlines|Iberia]], [[Hainan Airlines]], [[Kenya Airways]], [[South African Airways]], [[TAP Air Portugal]] and several regional carriers.  In 2003, domestic and international carriers carried 198,000 passengers.
